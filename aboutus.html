About the Troop

We are a troop made up of many scouts and have awarded many Eagle scouts.

Why should I join Troop?

  • Monthly Camping trips
  • Weekly Troop Meetings
  • Recognition for advancement in the troop

You must be 11 years old to join a boy scout troop. There are many scouts all over the world, and it is a very prestigious, significant, and fun-filled program to be part of. If you live near Hasbrouck Heights New Jersey and interested in joining a boy scout troop, feel free to contact us!
